CMS has opened up a large amount of data for public access and analysis. Below is a portal to the 2014 Medicare Utilization dataset that includes a 5% sample of all Medicare provider claims on record. For this project, we look at prostate cancer screening instances (Biopsies/PSA/DREs) to get an understanding of the frequency at which men are screened in various regions of the United States. The next step is to identify which states are the best suited to create pathology labs that require minimal shipping of samples. The following problem sets explore different optimiation problems considering the case of setting up one more multiple pathology labs either sequentially or simultaneously and the total amount of shipping involved in each arrangement.
